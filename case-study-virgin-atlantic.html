Virgin Atlantic: Revamping the Booking Process

Post-COVID booking experience addressing new travel regulations and customer expectations

Client

Virgin Atlantic

Role

Senior/Lead Product Designer

Duration

12 months

Team

Product team of 12

User Research Journey Mapping COVID Response Conversion Optimization

Project Overview

Virgin Atlantic needed to revamp their online booking process and homepage to adapt to new travel regulations and customer expectations post-COVID. The goal was to create a seamless, user-friendly experience that addressed new concerns around travel safety and convenience while maintaining the brand's distinctive personality. The redesign focused on simplifying the booking journey, providing clear information about travel restrictions, and reassuring customers about flexible booking policies.

Challenges

  • Simplifying Complex Booking Process: The existing booking flow involved multiple steps and complex decision points that created friction for users. The challenge was to streamline this process while still collecting all necessary information.

  • Clear COVID Communication: With constantly changing travel restrictions and requirements, it was essential to provide clear, up-to-date information without overwhelming users or creating anxiety.

  • Homepage Reassurance: The homepage needed to balance promotional content with reassuring messaging about safety measures and flexible booking policies to address customer concerns.

UX Process

  • 1

    Comprehensive User Research: We conducted surveys and interviews with frequent flyers and potential customers to understand their concerns, expectations, and pain points when booking travel post-COVID. This research revealed key insights about information needs and decision factors.

  • 2

    Competitor Analysis: We analyzed how other airlines were addressing similar challenges to identify best practices and opportunities for differentiation. This helped establish benchmarks for the redesign.

  • 3

    Website Analytics Review: Analysis of user behavior on the existing site helped identify drop-off points and areas of confusion, allowing us to prioritize high-impact improvements.

  • 4

    Iterative Design and Testing: We developed multiple design concepts and tested them with users, refining the approach based on feedback. This iterative process ensured that the final design met user needs effectively.

Design Solutions

Outcomes & Impact

  • Higher conversion rates achieved

  • Improved user satisfaction scores

  • Increased engagement with booking process

  • Successfully addressed post-COVID travel concerns
