Nutrimum: Supporting Mothers with Nutrition

Positioning cereal bars as convenient supplement alternative for maternal health

Client

Nutrimum

Role

UX Designer

Duration

4 months

Team

Product and content team of 6

Health Tech Maternal UX Trust Building Journey Mapping

Project Overview

Nutrimum aimed to position its cereal bars as a convenient alternative to traditional supplements for pregnant and breastfeeding mothers. The goal was to make it easier for women to fortify their diets during crucial stages of motherhood. The project involved creating a user-centered digital experience that provided clear nutritional information, stage-specific guidance, and built trust with the target audience.

Challenges

  • Building Trust: Establishing credibility in the maternal health space was crucial, as mothers are particularly discerning about nutritional products during pregnancy and breastfeeding.

  • Stage-Specific Needs: Nutritional needs vary significantly between pregnancy and breastfeeding stages. The platform needed to address these differences clearly and accurately.

  • Educational Content: Providing clear, scientifically accurate nutritional information and benefits without overwhelming users was a delicate balance to achieve.

UX Process

  • 1

    Persona Development: We created detailed personas for pregnant and breastfeeding mothers, capturing their unique concerns, motivations, and information needs at different stages of motherhood.

  • 2

    Journey Mapping: We mapped the emotional and physical journey of mothers through pregnancy and breastfeeding to identify key moments where nutritional support would be most valuable.

  • 3

    Trust-Building Content: We developed transparent, evidence-based content about the nutritional benefits of Nutrimum products, including clear information about ingredients and their specific benefits for maternal health.

  • 4

    Usability Testing: Multiple rounds of testing with pregnant and breastfeeding mothers helped refine the navigation and content presentation to ensure it met their specific needs.

Design Solutions

Outcomes & Impact

  • 35% increase in user engagement

  • Boost in user satisfaction scores

  • Successful positioning as supplement alternative

  • Tailored navigation appreciated by target audience
