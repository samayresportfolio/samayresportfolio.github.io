Project Overview

Vodafone aimed to simplify and enhance the self-service experience for customers through the launch of the eCare Reinvent dashboard. This platform was designed to empower users to manage their accounts and services without relying on traditional support channels. The project involved a comprehensive redesign of the customer portal, focusing on clarity, visibility, and personalization to create an intuitive and efficient self-service experience.

Challenges

  • Complex Service Journeys: Vodafone offers a wide range of services with complex billing structures and account management options. Creating a user-friendly interface for these high-demand services was a significant challenge.

  • Reducing Support Dependency: A key goal was to reduce the volume of calls and chats to customer support by providing intuitive self-service options. This required anticipating user needs and potential pain points.

  • Billing Clarity: Telecommunications billing is notoriously complex and often confusing for customers. Making this information easily understandable was a critical challenge.

UX Process

  • 1

    Comprehensive Platform Redesign: The redesign focused on three key principles: clarity, visibility, and personalization. These principles guided all design decisions throughout the project.

  • 2

    Billing Transparency: We redesigned the billing section to display upcoming bills with clear usage breakdowns, making it easier for customers to understand their charges and manage their accounts.

  • 3

    Enhanced Visibility: We emphasized future bill dates and charges, giving customers better visibility into their financial commitments and helping them plan accordingly.

  • 4

    Personalized Services: We introduced friendly service names for better customer experience, making technical services more approachable and easier to understand.

Design Solutions
