British Airways: Digital COVID Documentation

Streamlined digital solution for COVID-related document collection and verification

Client

British Airways

Role

Senior/Lead Product Designer

Duration

6 months

Team

Agile team of 8

Service Design MVP Development Global Scaling Digital Transformation

Project Overview

In response to evolving COVID-19 health regulations, British Airways sought to implement digital solutions to streamline the process of collecting essential COVID-related documents and improve overall passenger experience. The project focused on developing a user-friendly system that would allow passengers to upload and verify their COVID documentation before arriving at the airport, reducing congestion and stress during the check-in process.

Challenges

  • Dynamic Regulatory Landscape: COVID travel requirements were constantly changing, varying by country and sometimes by region. The solution needed to be flexible enough to adapt to these changes quickly.

  • Airport Congestion: Manual document checks were causing significant delays and congestion at airports. The digital solution needed to reduce this bottleneck while ensuring compliance with regulations.

  • Global Implementation: After initial success with the MVP in India, the solution needed to be scaled globally, accommodating different languages, document types, and regional requirements.

UX Process

  • 1

    End-to-End Service Design: We mapped the entire passenger journey, from booking to boarding, to identify key touchpoints where digital documentation could improve the experience. This holistic approach ensured that the solution was user-centric and efficient.

  • 2

    Nudge Theory Implementation: We designed subtle prompts and reminders throughout the journey to guide users through the document submission process, increasing completion rates and reducing last-minute stress.

  • 3

    MVP Development: We launched an initial version in India to demonstrate feasibility and gather real-world feedback. This approach allowed us to refine the solution before global rollout.

  • 4

    Agile Adaptation: Working in two-week sprints, we continuously adapted the solution to accommodate changing regulations and user feedback, ensuring that the system remained relevant and effective.

Design Solutions

Outcomes & Impact

  • Significantly reduced airport congestion

  • Enhanced overall travel experience

  • Positive feedback from travelers and industry experts

  • Successful global scaling from MVP
