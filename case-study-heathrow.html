Project Overview

Heathrow Airport aimed to use mapping as a tool to enhance passenger awareness and reduce anxiety for its 80 million annual passengers. The project focused on creating a unified, user-centric wayfinding solution that would help passengers navigate one of the world's busiest airports with confidence. The goal was to transform the passenger experience by providing clear, real-time information through both digital and physical touchpoints.

Challenges

  • Inconsistent Mapping Legacy: Heathrow had various mapping approaches across different terminals and systems. Unifying these disparate designs into a cohesive, user-friendly format was a major challenge.

  • Passenger Stress: Time constraints and uncertainty about navigation heighten passenger stress. The solution needed to provide reassurance and clear guidance to reduce anxiety.

  • Complex Transportation Hub: Heathrow's scale and complexity, with multiple terminals, transport links, and services, required a sophisticated yet intuitive navigation system.

UX Process

  • 1

    Hub-Map Project: We consolidated various designs into a unified, user-friendly format that could work across all terminals and platforms. This involved extensive analysis of existing systems and user needs.

  • 2

    Extensive Usability Testing: We conducted comprehensive testing with diverse passenger groups to develop a standardized mapping solution that worked for all user types, from frequent flyers to first-time visitors.

  • 3

    Digital Innovation: We created a map-based interface that provided real-time information about flight gates, delays, services, and amenities, empowering passengers with up-to-date information.

  • 4

    Cross-Platform Implementation: The solution was deployed across physical terminals, online platforms, and mobile applications to ensure consistent wayfinding experiences across all touchpoints.

Design Solutions

Outcomes & Impact

  • Smoother navigation for all passengers

  • Reduced passenger stress and anxiety

  • Empowered passengers with real-time information

  • Set new standards for airport navigation
