Project Overview

Tesco Real Food serves 3.7 million users monthly as the UK's third-largest recipe site. The project focused on enhancing user engagement through data-led innovation and real-time content creation. By leveraging user insights and implementing interactive tools, the platform aimed to provide a more personalized and engaging experience for its diverse audience while extending its influence beyond the website through multichannel distribution.

Challenges

  • Maintaining High Engagement: With a diverse audience of 3.7 million monthly users, keeping content fresh, relevant, and engaging was a significant challenge.

  • Real-Time Content Creation: Developing content that responds to current trends, seasonal events, and user preferences required a nimble approach to content strategy.

  • Expanding Reach: Increasing influence beyond website users to enhance brand perception among non-users was a key challenge for the multichannel strategy.

UX Process

  • 1

    Data-Led Innovation: We utilized meal planners, calculators, and interactive tools to gather insights about user preferences and behaviors. This data informed content strategy and feature development.

  • 2

    Real-Time Content Strategy: A dedicated newsroom team leveraged insights from user data to create timely, relevant content that responded to current trends and seasonal events.

  • 3

    Interactive Tools Implementation: We developed and refined interactive tools that encouraged user participation and provided valuable data for personalization and content strategy.

  • 4

    Multichannel Distribution: Content was strategically distributed across social media, email, and other channels to extend reach beyond the website and enhance brand perception.

Design Solutions

Outcomes & Impact

  • Significantly boosted user engagement

  • +3% perception increase among non-website users

  • Enhanced personalized user experience

  • Strengthened position as leading UK recipe site
